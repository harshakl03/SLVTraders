Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/09/2024

At SLV Traders, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard your data when you interact with our website, mobile app, or in-store services.

When you visit our website or place an order, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping or billing address when you make a purchase or sign up for an account.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process your orders and deliver products.

  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Improve our website and shopping experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except for:

  • Service Providers: We work with third-party vendors (e.g., payment processors, delivery services) to fulfill orders and ensure secure transactions.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to remember your preferences and provide a smoother shopping experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, though some features may be limited if cookies are disabled.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmitting or storing data online is 100% secure.

6. Changes to the Terms

We reserve the right to modify these Terms of Use at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on the website. We encourage you to review the terms periodically to stay informed.
